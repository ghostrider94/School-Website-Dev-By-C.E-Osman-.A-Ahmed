Admissions

Join our community of learners and future leaders

Application Process

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form and submit required documents including transcripts and recommendations.

2

Campus Visit

Schedule a tour of our facilities and meet with our admissions team to learn more about our programs.

3

Assessment & Interview

Participate in age-appropriate assessments and meet with faculty members for a personal interview.

4

Decision & Enrollment

Receive your admission decision and complete enrollment paperwork to secure your spot.

Application Requirements

Application Form

Completed online application with student information and family details

Transcripts

Official academic records from previous schools (if applicable)

Recommendations

Two letters of recommendation from teachers or community leaders

Birth Certificate

Copy of student's birth certificate or proof of age

Health Records

Immunization records and health examination form

Application Fee

$50 non-refundable application processing fee

Tuition & Financial Aid

2025-2026 Tuition Rates

Elementary (K-5) $15,000/year
Middle School (6-8) $18,000/year
High School (9-12) $20,000/year

Tuition includes textbooks, technology fees, and most extracurricular activities.

Financial Aid Available

Bright Future Academy is committed to making quality education accessible to all qualified students. We offer:

  • Need-based financial aid packages
  • Merit scholarships for exceptional students
  • Sibling discounts (10% for additional children)
  • Flexible payment plans available

Over 40% of our students receive some form of financial assistance. Applications are reviewed confidentially.
