Frequently Asked Questions

What are your admission requirements?

We require a completed application form, transcripts, two letters of recommendation, birth certificate, health records, and a $50 application fee. Visit our Admissions page for complete details.

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes! We offer need-based financial aid, merit scholarships, and sibling discounts. Over 40% of our students receive some form of financial assistance.

What is your student-to-teacher ratio?

Our elementary classes have 15-20 students, while middle and high school classes typically have 18-22 students, ensuring personalized attention for each student.

Can I schedule a campus tour?

Absolutely! Contact our admissions office at (555) 123-4568 or use the contact form above to schedule a personalized tour of our campus.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer over 30 clubs and activities including sports teams, arts programs, academic clubs, and service organizations. Check our Programs page for a complete list.
