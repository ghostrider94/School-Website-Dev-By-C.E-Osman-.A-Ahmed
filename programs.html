Academic Programs

Elementary Program

Grades K-5

Our elementary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, mathematics, science, and social studies. We emphasize hands-on learning and creative exploration.

  • Project-based learning
  • Small class sizes (15-20 students)
  • Integrated arts education
  • Character development program

Middle School Program

Grades 6-8

The middle school program prepares students for higher-level academics while supporting their social and emotional development during these critical years.

  • Advanced STEM curriculum
  • Elective courses available
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Advisory programs

High School Program

Grades 9-12

Our high school program offers rigorous academics, AP courses, and college prep to prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.

  • 15+ AP courses offered
  • College counseling services
  • Internship opportunities
  • Dual enrollment programs
